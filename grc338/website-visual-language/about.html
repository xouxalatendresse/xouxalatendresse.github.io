Welcome to La Petite Maison, a culinary haven where French gastronomy meets contemporary elegance. Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, our restaurant offers a sophisticated dining experience that celebrates the rich tapestry of French cuisine. With a commitment to using only the finest ingredients sourced locally and from France, our talented chefs craft each dish with precision and passion.

Step into our charming establishment and embark on a journey through the diverse flavors of France. From classic dishes like Coq au Vin and Beef Bourguignon to innovative creations that push the boundaries of traditional French cooking, our menu is a reflection of the country's culinary heritage and creativity. Whether you're seeking a romantic dinner for two or a celebratory feast with friends and family, La Petite Maison invites you to savor the artistry of French cooking in an inviting ambiance.
